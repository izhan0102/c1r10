Muskurahat Foundation is a registered Indian Educational Non-Profit Organisation (NGO) under the Mumbai Public Trust Act, 1950. We are a 12A registered society with section 80(G) status of Income Tax Act, 1961.

Muskurahat complies with all financial regulations applicable for non-profits organisations in India. Our financial accounts are audited annually and published on our website.

Our Vision

Future-Proofing the next generation, today.

Future-proofing (verb): Equipping the next generation with knowledge and skills to not only survive but also thrive in a dynamic world, ensuring their long-term success and well-being.

Our Mission

With every child

To be the consistent support for every child in their journey towards becoming happy, independent, and socially, responsible citizens.

For every child

To advocate for every child by mobilizing individuals, communities, government and institutions to act.

Our Journey

In 2014, Himanshu Goenka, then a 17 year old, visited a shelter home to spend a day with the children to bring some happiness in their lives. His experience fostered the desire to do this for the rest of his life.

Over the last 10 years, Muskurahat has grown from a group of young volunteers bringing smiles to children in need to an impact driven education NGO that is striving to provide access to quality education and a chance for a better future to all children in need.

A decade of striving to bring Muskurahat to every child.
